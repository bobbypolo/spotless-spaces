Our Services

Tailored cleaning for homes and businesses across Lake County

🏡

Recurring Home Cleaning

Consistent upkeep on a weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly schedule. Every visit covers dusting, vacuuming, mopping, and refreshing kitchens and bathrooms — keeping your home effortlessly clean between deep cleans, on a rhythm that fits your life.

🧽

Deep Cleaning

Our most thorough, top-to-bottom reset. We hand-clean stovetops and microwaves inside and out, wipe down refrigerator exteriors and cabinet faces, and scrub showers, tubs, toilets, baseboards, blinds, and ceiling fans — every inch sanitized and shining. Ideal as a first visit or a seasonal refresh.

📦

Move In / Move Out

Starting fresh or handing over the keys? We clean empty homes from top to bottom — inside cabinets and drawers, ovens and appliances, closets, and floors — so your old place is rent- or sale-ready and your new one is spotless before the boxes arrive. Great for tenants, homeowners, and realtors.

🏢

Commercial Cleaning

Reliable, professional cleaning for offices, salons, studios, retail shops, and other small-business spaces. We keep reception areas, restrooms, break rooms, floors, and high-touch surfaces consistently spotless — working around your hours so your business stays welcoming for staff and customers without missing a beat.

🔑

Vacation Rental & Turnover

Fast, reliable turnovers between guests. We reset the whole space — beds made with fresh linens, bathrooms and kitchen sanitized, trash removed, and essentials restocked — so your short-term rental or Airbnb is five-star ready for the next check-in, every time.

🌷

One-Time & Spring Cleaning

No recurring plan needed. A single, comprehensive clean for spring refreshes, holidays, hosting guests, or simply catching up — all the thoroughness of our deep clean, scheduled whenever it suits you.
